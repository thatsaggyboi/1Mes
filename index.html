Steps to install

1 Download Rom

Download the port rom and place it inside the Internal storage.

2 Wipe Everything

Wipe everything on both slots except internal storage.

3 Flash Rom

Flash port rom using any custom recovery and then reboot. Flash additonal zips/magisk after first boot.

4 Enjoy Rom :)

First boot will take some time so be patient. Enjoy ! ! !

* This procedure is standard. It may vary device to device. Do not report any bugs without applying additional fixes.
