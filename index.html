Steps to install

1 Download Rom

Download the port rom and place rom inside the Internal storage

2 Wipe Everything

Wipe everything except internal. clean flash recommended but dirty flash also works fine.

3 Flash Rom

Flash port rom using any custom recovery and then reboot. Flash additonal zips/magisk after first boot.

4 Enjoy Rom :)

First boot will take some time so be patient. Don't report bugs without applying additional fixes.

* Above procedure is standard. it may affect differet for other Devices.
